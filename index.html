About the Symposium

Ampheroz is the annual symposium of the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, bringing together innovation, creativity, and fun under one roof. With a perfect blend of technical competitions, non-technical events, and interactive workshops, Ampheroz offers a dynamic platform for students to showcase their talents and explore new ideas.
